The OpenClaw ecosystem is growing rapidly. From productivity tools to developer plugins, there's something for everyone.

## What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open-source AI assistant platform that runs on your own hardware. It connects to chat apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, and Slack, and can automate tasks, manage your calendar, and assist with coding.
