Getting your product listed in the BuiltWithOpenClaw directory is free and straightforward. Here's how to do it.

## Prerequisites

Your product must integrate with or extend OpenClaw. This includes:

- SaaS applications that use OpenClaw
- Plugins and extensions
- Skills and custom capabilities
- Tools built for the OpenClaw ecosystem

## Submission Steps

1. **Visit the submit page** - Go to [builtwithopenclaw.com/submit](/submit)

2. **Fill out the form** - Provide your product name, URL, tagline, description, category, and contact email

3. **Add proof** - Include a link or description showing how your product uses OpenClaw

4. **Submit** - We'll review your submission and get back to you within a few days

## What Happens Next?

Our team manually reviews each submission. We check that:

- The product genuinely integrates with OpenClaw
- The listing is accurate and complete
- The product meets our quality standards

Approved listings appear in our directory and can be discovered by thousands of OpenClaw users.
