OpenClaw has grown into a powerful ecosystem of plugins that extend its capabilities. Whether you're a developer, marketer, or power user, these plugins can help you get more done.

## Why Use OpenClaw Plugins?

Plugins integrate directly with your OpenClaw instance, giving you access to new skills, integrations, and automation capabilities. They're easy to install and configure.

## Our Top Picks

1. **Productivity plugins** - Automate repetitive tasks and streamline your workflow
2. **Developer tools** - Code assistance, Git integration, and deployment helpers
3. **Communication** - Connect to Slack, Discord, and email
4. **Analytics** - Track usage and get insights
5. **Custom skills** - Build your own capabilities
